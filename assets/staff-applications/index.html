UltraVanilla staff applications

UltraVanilla is a server that relies on an active staff team to enforce rules in a three year old world with no claims or block protection. We're looking for serious people who take the responsibilities seriously, but who are also participants in the community the way normal players are.

Do not ask staff to read your application. There are typically 20-40 people who apply — and we get around to reading every one of them.
