UltraVanilla staff applications

UltraVanilla is a server that relies on an active staff team to enforce rules in a four year old world with no claims or block protection. We're looking for serious people who take the responsibilities seriously, but who are also participants in the community the way normal players are.

Do not ask staff to read your application. There are typically 10-20 people who apply — and we get around to reading every one of them.

Scenario questions

Hint: For each one, we recommend considering the following aspects:

  • Is it actionable at all?
  • How would you go about investigating and collecting more details, if needed? How would you go about documenting and communicating with other staff? Is there any ambiguity?
  • What is the proper characterization of the actions? (What is it called when someone does this?)
  • What actions can be done to deal with the behavior? (This may be conditional based on evidence!)

SCENARIO 1: Someone starts breaking signs and burning the maps at spawn.

SCENARIO 2: Someone who joined yesterday removed some items out of chests in a town.

SCENARIO 3: You notice that the shopping district has been stolen from, some guy took like 50 diamonds from two shops. This person has been playing for a couple days now and doesn't seem to be a troll.

SCENARIO 4: An owner of a town reports that their friend dug a massive hole in their town. This town owner told their friend they want the hole filled in, but they refused. How might you communicate with the involved parties to resolve this?

SCENARIO 5: There is some banter going on between two individuals and they happen to say something very insulting or offensive to each other. Neither of them are actually upset about it though.

SCENARIO 6: Someone says "islam is poisoning europe and must be removed from public life, it is incompatible with the west" in a conversation.

SCENARIO 7: Someone struggling with blazes says "these MOTHERFUCKING blazes refuse to die".

SCENARIO 8: In a conversation about various conspiracy theories and political topics, someone says "the cuban government used a directed acoustic energy weapon against the US embassy in 2016".

SCENARIO 9: The admins are being too strict, or too laid back. Or you have a problem with another staff member doing something disagreeable.
